The Persona behind the Beats

Operating as an underground producer means stripping away artificial industry filters and putting raw truth straight into the master track. For Xren, identity is not a footnote—it is an active processing engine for sound design.

Living Openly Transgender: Navigating digital subcultures, hyperpop currents, and digicore soundscapes while embracing an open trans journey allows Xren to discard traditional boxes. Every track reflects personal evolution, capturing both the friction and absolute freedom of transition.

Intersection: Faith & Fluidity

Where many people assume lines must be drawn, Xren constructs bridges. Holding onto a profound personal foundation in Christianity while completely owning an LGBTQ+ identity presents an artistic space that subverts expectation.

Rather than conforming to modern labels or rigid external perspectives, Xren channels this unique spiritual and cultural intersection directly into electronic composition. It is high-energy, unapologetic music crafted by an artist secure in who they are created to be.

A Safe Node for Digital Outcasts

The underground scene thrives because it gives shelter to individuals who don't fit normal frequencies. Xrenbeats projects are intentionally curated to establish a safe, sonic workspace for transgender youth, queer artists, and anyone searching for community in the digital wilderness.
